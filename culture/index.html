Life at flaconi

We Deliver Moments of Beauty That Spark Joy

This vision drives us. It’s not only about the individual moments and the feelings they evoke – though those are central – but also about the we behind it all.

#WeCommerce is what we do. For us, this means bringing customers, colleagues, and partners together in harmony. This core idea shapes our decisions, our actions, and our company culture.

With our Code of Ethics, company values, and Leadership Standards, we have created key building blocks that help us continuously foster this core principle. These cultural pillars provide the framework and strengthen all our initiatives, making flaconi an exceptional place to be.

The way we work

To empower our employees to achieve optimal work-life balance, we developed three flexible working models:

#Traveller

Our hybrid model combines the freedom of remote work with the benefits of office presence. flaconis who choose this model work primarily remote but come into the office for eight days per month. How you distribute these days is entirely up to you, offering the flexibility to ensure that work fits your life, not the other way around.

#Settler

This model is perfect for those who prefer the office as their primary workspace and value direct, in-person interaction. You will work on-site four days a week, with the flexibility to work remotely one day a week. This way, you get the full office experience while maintaining the freedom to shape your schedule.

#Astronaut

Our remote model is perfect for flaconis who live outside of Berlin and prefer to work entirely from home. As we foster an “In-Person-First” culture, this model is available for selected individual contributor roles. The only requirement is that your place of residence and work must be located within Germany.

Responsibility

Every company must take responsibility for the impact of its actions—whether social, environmental, or economic. At flaconi, we take all aspects of sustainability seriously, as we are committed to actively and responsibly shaping the future of our industry.

Environmental Responsibility

Developing innovative packaging, avoiding plastic, and ensuring products arrive safely and resource-efficiently is a challenge we embrace. Today, our flaconi shipping boxes are 100% plastic-free. As a member of the Forest Stewardship Council (FSC), we are committed to using 100% recycled packaging materials.

Our commitment extends to our infrastructure: Our logistics center runs on 100% renewable energy, and we fully offset the emissions from our heating systems. At our headquarters, we further reduce our carbon footprint through Smart Heating and Smart Lighting solutions.

Social Responsibility

Diversity makes us better: it opens up new perspectives and allows everyone to discover their true self and embrace their individuality. Our culture is shaped by openness, tolerance, and curiosity. We actively promote talent and diversity while prioritizing the physical and mental health and overall well-being of our colleagues.

Our Vision for the Future

We believe in the skills and dedication of our teams to develop innovative ideas for a shared future. As a company, we are fully aware of our responsibility to make sustainable decisions at every level and to actively stand up for a diverse society. We are working hard to empower our customers to make sustainable purchasing decisions that respect both nature and people alike.

This is just the beginning. We are constantly striving to find innovative and sustainable solutions for our colleagues, partners, and customers. We certainly won’t get everything perfect right away—but as “flaconis,” we are determined to do our part. Because great goals can only be achieved together.
