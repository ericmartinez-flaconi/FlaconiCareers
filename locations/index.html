Locations

Around 700 colleagues work together across our two core locations, united in our mission to serve over 3.6 million active customers with the best possible shopping experience.

Additionally, we offer our customers a unique offline experience. Our flaconi Store features an innovative concept with a showroom atmosphere, where you can experience a curated portfolio of luxury, niche, and hair brands up close.

Berlin

It’s the right mix!

Our headquarters in the heart of Berlin-Charlottenburg brings together all key business, tech, and brand functions. In our modern office, “The Benjamin,” overlooking the Spree River, more than 5,050 m² offer space for innovation, collaboration, and team spirit – a place not just to work, but to shape the future together.

Halle

The heart of our operations!

In April 2021, we opened our logistics center “Polarstern” in Star Park near Halle (Saale). On a total area of 28,000 m², we process around 60,000 orders daily, supported by a state-of-the-art, semi-automated storage system. Located directly off the A14, this industrial and commercial area is easily accessible by car and public transportation.

Our innovative storage system and the dedicated team on-site make this workplace truly unique.

Beauty in it’s own fashion!
