<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Halle</h2>



<p><strong>The heart of our operations!</strong></p>



<p>In April 2021, we opened our logistics center “Polarstern” in Star Park near Halle (Saale). On a total area of 28,000 m², we process around 60,000 orders daily, supported by a state-of-the-art, semi-automated storage system. Located directly off the A14, this industrial and commercial area is easily accessible by car and public transportation.</p>



<p>Our innovative storage system and the dedicated team on-site make this workplace truly unique. </p>
