Your Career

#WeCommerce thrives on our partners, our customers, and especially our colleagues. We are excited that you are interested in flaconi as an employer and look forward to getting to know you during the recruitment process.

Recruitment Process Overview

    Welcome to flaconi!

    In the final step, you will receive all the essential information for your first day. We give you a warm welcome and guide you through your tasks step by step. Your colleagues will be right there to support you during your first few weeks, ensuring you feel at home and ready to succeed.

    Step 6/6

    Online Application

    You submit your application through our career portal.

    Step 1/6

    Application Review

    We review your documents in coordination with the relevant department.

    Step 2/6

    Getting to Know Each Other

    If your application stands out, you’ll begin your personalized recruitment journey with us. Because every role is unique, we tailor the steps to fit the specific position. We’ll provide you with all the necessary details along the way, so you always know what to expect next. Throughout the process, you’ll have the opportunity to meet your potential future colleagues and lead.

    Step 3/6

    We Choose Each Other!

    If we both decide that it’s a match, you will receive a job offer from us. We believe in a personal touch, so we will first contact you via video call to share the good news and discuss the details before sending over your official offer letter.

    Step 4/6

    All Details Clear

    Once all the details regarding your start are finalized, we’ll begin preparing your onboarding. We make sure everything is ready so you can hit the ground running and feel right at home in your new team from day one.
